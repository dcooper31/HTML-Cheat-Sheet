HTML Cheat Sheet

Headings

Headings are defined with the <h1> to <h6> tags.

Paragraphs

Paragraphs are defined with the <p> tag.

Lists

Unordered Lists

Unordered Lists are marked with bullet points and go in 'ul' tags

Types of Lists:

  • Unordered List
  • Ordered List

Anchors

Anchors are used to link to other pages or parts of the same page. They are defined with the <a> tag.
